# Engagement Scene

# Preparation

## Like, Follow, Share (~5s)
Say exactly like this "Before moving on, please Like, Follow, and Share to help the channel and other people grow". Show three white rounded-rectangle buttons in a row, each a white card with a coloured bold label: Like (bold black), Follow (bold red), Share (bold blue). Keep these three button labels in ENGLISH — "Like", "Follow", "Share" — in BOTH the Indonesian and the English version (do NOT translate the button words; only the spoken narration is in the scene's language). For the icons, use a real Unicode CHARACTER GLYPH from the bundled Noto Sans font — a ♥ (U+2665) heart beside the Like label, and an arrow glyph such as ➜ or ↗ beside the Share label. Do NOT hand-draw any icon shapes (no Polygon/Arc heart, bell, or arrow). The bundled font has no clean glyph for a bell or a thumbs-up, so the Follow button shows its text only, no icon.
The scene is for after the first scene.
Do NOT include any thank-you / closing "thank you" / "terima kasih" line — neither spoken in the narration nor shown on screen.
